Fried Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes into thin rounds or wedges.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the potatoes to the skillet and season with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cook for 10-15 minutes, flipping occasionally, until the potatoes are golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot.
