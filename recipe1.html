Chapatis

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Extra flour for rolling

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft dough.
  2. Add vegetable oil and knead for another 5 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle using a rolling pin.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook each chapati for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Brush with a little oil if desired and serve warm.
